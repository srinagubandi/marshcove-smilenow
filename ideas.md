## Selected Approach

**Approach 1: Florida Coastal Luxury** — This best matches Marsh Cove's existing brand identity (green + gold + marsh imagery) while delivering the high-urgency conversion elements needed for paid ad landing pages. The warm, premium feel builds trust with the 50-70 age demographic in Naples/Fort Myers.

### Chosen Design System
- **Primary Green**: #1E4620 (deep forest)
- **Gold Accent**: #C4922A (warm amber)
- **Background**: #FAFAF7 (warm cream)
- **Section Alt**: #F0F4EC (sage tint)
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display, italic for emotional phrases
- **Body**: Lato, 16px, 1.6 line height
- **CTA Buttons**: Gold background, dark green text, rounded-full, bold
- **Mobile**: Single column, sticky call bar, large tap targets (min 48px)
